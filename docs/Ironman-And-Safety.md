# Ironman And Safety Notes

## Ironman Mode

Ironman mode disables Grand Exchange actions. This includes restocking and selling.

## Stable Modes

Simple and Advanced sawmill modes are the primary stable workflows.

## Experimental Modes

These are optional and should be tested carefully:

- GE restocking
- GE selling
- Lunar Plank Make
- Lunar altar switching
- Lunar world hopping

## Recommended Safety Practice

Start with owned supplies and confirm the route works for your account before enabling experimental options.
